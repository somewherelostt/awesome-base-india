## About

Digital Duniya Podcast is an educational platform that delves into the world of web3, blockchain, and crypto, with a special focus on simplifying complex topics for a Hindi-speaking and regional language audience in India. Hosted by Apoorva, the podcast covers news, deep dives, and interviews in multiple languages, including Hindi and other regional languages. It aims to make emerging technologies accessible to a broader audience while empowering them with the knowledge to understand and engage with the rapidly evolving digital world.
